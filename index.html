Hey there!

I'm Drew Fulton, a full-stack developer based in sunny Philadelphia, who enjoys developing creative web apps that are both functional and intuitive. Admittedly, I'm obsessed with anything web or tech related, particularly new technologies that help simplify day-to-day life.

My favorite part of programming is the never-ending sense that there's always something new to master --- a secret language feature, an established design pattern, an emerging library or -- most importantly -- a different way of looking at a problem. It's like being an artist who gains a new set of paintbrushes with every hour of effort.

Prior to becoming a web developer, I was an account executive and project manager at various advertising agencies in Philadelphia and New Jersey. For 5 years I worked as the client's point of contact as we developed print and digital marketing campaigns for globally recognized consumer and healthcare brands. I enjoyed developing and adhering to process, interfacing with clients, and reveled in the satisfaction of seeing a new campaign come to life.
